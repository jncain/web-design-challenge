Grade 8 Mathematics

The graph displays the percent of 8th grade students in Minnesota and the Nation (National public) performing at or above NAEP Proficient for mathematics from 2003 to 2019.

For 8th grade mathematics, performing at the NAEP Proficient level consists of applying mathematical concepts and procedures consistently to complex problems in the five NAEP content areas, which are number properties and operations; measurement; geometry; data analysis, statistics, and probability; and algebra.
